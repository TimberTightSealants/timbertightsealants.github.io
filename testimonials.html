We make sure to get it right

We've reached out to some of our customers to share their honest opinions with us about their experience. Timber Tight takes pride in its fondness for its community and is continually looking to improve so that we can serve you better.

Barb & Christine - Chandos Lake
"Such a kind person!! Really big part of our community, not only does Josh run Timber Tight Sealants, but also coaches hockey and helps at the firehall - conversation goes a long way!! We asked Josh to come out to our cottage on Chandos after we had a new sliding door and deck installed facing the lake - had it sealed the same day we called and colour matched our current caulking. Trying to find excuses to hire him again!!"

We plan on using Timber Tight here this coming season, book him before we do!"

Luanne & Randy - Stoney Lake
