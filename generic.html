About Timber Tight

Locally Owned & Operated since 2024

What We Do

Since opening our doors in 2024, we've been dedicated to providing reliable, high-quality caulking services for residential clients. Timber Tight prides itself on precision workmanship, durable results, and a commitment to customer satisifaction. Whether its sealing, waterproofing, or restoration, we deliver solutions that protect your property and stand the test of time.

Investing in new caulking applications offers numerous benefits for any property. Fresh, professionally applied caulking helps prevent drafts, water intrusion, and moisture damage - one of the leading causes of structural deterioration and mold growth. It also improves energy efficiency by sealing gaps and joints, reducing heating and cooling loss throughout the year. Beyond protection, new caulking enhances the overall appearance of a building by creating clean, seamless lines around windows, doors, siding, and interior finishes. With modern materials designed for long-lasting flexibility and durability, a new application provides peace of mind and lasting value for your home or business.



Our Commitment

As we look toward the future of building, restoration, and envelope protection, our commitment remains clear: to deliver caulking and sealing solutions that stand the test of time. We move forward as a technologically equipped company, embracing modern tools, materials, and methods that enhance precision, durability, and overall project efficiency. Our team is skilled and well-trained, ensuring every application - from waterproofing to firestopping - is performed with craftsmenship, accuracy, and professionalism.

We continue to grow as a digitally mature organization, using digital tools to improve communication, estimating, scheduling, documentation, and client experience. And above all, we remain firmly safety-driven, ensuring that every site, every project, and every crew member operates under the highest safety standards in the industry.

Together, these strengths position us not only as a trusted caulking provider today, but as a future-ready partner committed to innovation, reliability, and long-lasting results.
